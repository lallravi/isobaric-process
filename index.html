Isobaric Expansion

  • Imagine heat (Q) supplied to a gas with a movable piston
  • Since the volume increases the gas does work by pushing the piston outward
  • Since the temperature (ΔT) increases the internal energy must increase (ΔU).
  • Thermal energy is added to the system (Q) to increase internal energy (ΔU) and to perform work (W).
W=PΔV Q=ΔU+W
Q > 0: Heat is added.
W > 0: Work is done by the gas.
ΔU > 0: Internal energy increases
